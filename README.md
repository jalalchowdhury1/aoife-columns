# Borrow & Carry 🧱

A little web game that teaches Aoife how **column subtraction and addition** work on
paper — borrowing ("knock next door!"), carrying the one, and the tricky
"write the missing digits" test puzzles.

**Play it here: https://aoife-columns.vercel.app**

## How it works

Eight levels, from single digits up to the detective puzzle from her test. Each level
has five buttons:

1. **👀 Watch** — the app solves one slowly, showing every move on the tower
2. **🤝 Together** — she answers each move, with hints when she slips
3. **🧭 Lead** — she picks *which move comes next*, then does it
4. **🦋 Solo** — a fresh problem, just her and the answer (confetti!)
5. **🔁 Practice** — five in a row; all five first-try earns the trophy

The tower updates live while she works — crossed-out digits, the little borrowed 1,
the carried +1 — exactly the way it looks on paper.

Progress saves on the device automatically. Parents: tap the title 5 times quickly to
see a progress report.

Works great on the iPad. No account, no ads, nothing to install.
